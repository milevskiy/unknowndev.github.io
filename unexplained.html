Android, iOS, Windows(Phone)

DESCRIPTION

The main character has the ability of telekinesis. Running from the zombie, he is at a dead end and he had no choice but to to survive. Features: 3D graphics, unique character abilities, different classes of zombies, 4 types of zombies, different type of energy, with a tap, you can tilt the zombies in different directions, boss is killed with a lot of touch.

The game has support for virtual reality.
